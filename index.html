The Golden State Warriors are a Bay Area team representing the Pacific Division and the Western Conference. Following years of development, the Warriors found a championship direction headed by the likes of Stephen Curry, Klay Thompson, Harrison Barnes, Andre Iguodala, and Draymond Green. Four time NBA Champions, the Warriors ended a 40 year Finals and Championship drought in 2015 defeating the Cleveland Cavaliers in a seven game series. The following season was a historic feat which ended in shattering the seemingly unbreakable 72-10 season record set by the Bulls in 1995-96. Though the season didn't end as per plan, the Warriors have retooled in order to regain their championship status for years to come following the free agent acquisitions of Kevin Durant, Zaza Pachulia, David West, and many others.
